MOVE: Mobility and Operation of Vehicle Electrification

Empowering the Future of Electric Mobility

EV Certification Program for Engineers, Planners & Managers

🔍 Program Overview

The MOVE program is a comprehensive EV certification course designed to build your skills in electric mobility, infrastructure, sustainability, policy, and business strategies. It’s tailored for engineers, policymakers, entrepreneurs, and fleet managers who want to make an impact in the growing EV industry.

📚 Program Curriculum

  • C-EVM001: Electric Vehicle Systems and Future of Mobility
  • C-EVM002: Powering Electric Vehicles: Infrastructure & Energy
  • C-EVM003: Sustainable Urban Mobility & Policy Planning
  • C-EVM004: Economics & Strategy for EV Adoption

🌟 Why Choose This Program?

  • Learn from top EV industry experts
  • Hands-on training with real-world case studies
  • Earn a professional certificate upon completion
  • Boost your career in the rapidly growing EV ecosystem
  • Lifetime access to course materials and resources

📈 Evaluation & Certification

Participants will be evaluated through quizzes, assignments, and a final project. A certificate of completion will be awarded to all participants scoring above 60%.
